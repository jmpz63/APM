# Shipping & Packaging Checklists — Mason Jar Gift Kits

Date: 2025-11-17

## Pack a Single Jar (D2C)
- Inspect glass and lid; add tamper seal if applicable
- Wrap jar: 2+ layers bubble, cap protection; place in inner carton with void fill
- Add recipe/instruction card; brand insert
- Seal inner carton; place inside outer shipper with cushioning on all sides
- Affix 4x6 label (ZPL/PDF); avoid seams and corners; cover existing markings

## Pack Multiple Jars (2/4/6)
- Use partitioned inserts or molded pulp dividers
- Confirm total weight and dimensional weight (DIM) vs. carrier rate
- Shake test and drop test from table height; add void fill as needed

## Labeling & Compliance
- Food jars: FDA 21 CFR 101 — Nutrition Facts, ingredients, allergens, net qty
- Non-food (candles/fragrance): GHS/CLP warnings as applicable
- FPLA: identity, responsibility, net contents on retail label
- Barcodes: GS1 UPC/GTIN or QR to instructions

## Shipping
- USPS/UPS/FedEx guidelines for fragile glass; insurance if needed
- Choose service based on DIM and destination; compare flat-rate vs. weight
- Photo of packed box and label; save tracking #

## Returns & Replacements
- Keep batch/lot code on labels; simple return policy
- Document breakage rate; update packaging design accordingly
