**Materials Needed**:
- Soy wax flakes or beeswax
- Candle wick (appropriate size for jar diameter)
- Wick stickers or hot glue
- Essential oils or candle fragrance
- Double boiler or microwave-safe container

**Steps**:

1. **Calculate wax**: Fill jar with wax flakes, pour into measuring cup. That's how much melted wax you need.

2. **Prepare jar**: 
   - Clean and dry completely
   - Attach wick to center of jar bottom with sticker/glue

3. **Melt wax**: 
   - Double boiler or microwave in 30-second intervals
   - Heat to 170-180Â°F

4. **Add fragrance**: 
   - Remove from heat
   - Add 1 oz fragrance per 1 lb wax
   - Stir gently 1-2 minutes

5. **Pour**: 
   - Let wax cool to 140-160Â°F
   - Pour into jar, leaving Â½" at top
   - Center and secure wick with clothespin across top

6. **Cool**: 
   - Let set 24 hours undisturbed
   - Trim wick to Â¼"

## â„ï¸ Snow Globe Template

**Jar Size**: 8-16 oz (shorter/wider jars work best)

**Materials**:
- Small waterproof figurine/decoration
- Waterproof glue (E6000 or epoxy)
- Distilled water
- Glycerin (1 tsp per cup of water)
- Fine glitter (1-2 tsp)

**Steps**:

1. **Glue figurine**: 
   - Clean lid thoroughly
   - Glue figurine to INSIDE of lid
   - Let cure 24 hours

2. **Fill jar**:
   - Fill jar almost to top with distilled water
   - Add glycerin (slows glitter fall)
   - Add glitter

3. **Test fit**: 
   - Screw on lid with figurine
   - Check water level (should be full with no air bubbles)
   - Adjust if needed

4. **Seal**: 
   - Apply waterproof glue around lid threads
   - Screw on tightly
   - Let cure 24 hours

5. **Decorate**: 
   - Paint lid exterior
   - Add ribbon around jar rim
   - Create themed base if desired

**Theme Ideas**:
- Holiday: Santa, snowman, Christmas tree
- Birthday: Number candles, confetti
- Travel: Miniature landmarks
- Wedding: Bride and groom figures

## ğŸ¨ Decoupage Decoration Template

**Jar Size**: Any size

**Materials**:
- Mod Podge (matte or glossy)
- Decorative paper, fabric, or napkins
- Foam brush
- Scissors
- Acrylic sealer (optional)

**Steps**:

1. **Prep surface**: Clean jar, remove labels, dry completely

2. **Cut designs**: 
   - Tissue paper, scrapbook paper, fabric
   - Photos, sheet music, magazine cutouts

3. **Apply Mod Podge**: 
   - Thin layer on jar surface
   - Place paper/fabric
   - Smooth out bubbles with brush
   - Apply Mod Podge over top

4. **Layer**: Continue until jar is covered as desired

5. **Seal**: 
   - Let dry 24 hours
   - Apply 2-3 coats of Mod Podge as sealer
   - Optional: Clear acrylic sealer spray

**Design Ideas**:
- Vintage sheet music for music lover
- Photos for memory jar
- Floral patterns for spring decor
- Comic book pages for pop culture fan
- Maps for travel theme
