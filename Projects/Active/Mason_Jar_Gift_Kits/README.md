## üí° Tips & Best Practices

### Preparation
- **Clean thoroughly**: Remove labels, wash with hot soapy water
- **Dry completely**: Prevent moisture issues
- **Sterilize for food**: Boil jars if using for edible gifts

### Presentation
- **Layer strategically**: Visual appeal for dry mixes
- **Color coordination**: Match ribbons and labels to theme
- **Clear instructions**: Include recipe/use cards
- **Professional labels**: Printed or handwritten neatly

### Storage
- **Keep cool and dry**: For food items
- **Avoid direct sunlight**: For items sensitive to UV
- **Stack safely**: Store in cabinets with careful organization

### Gifting
- **Personalization**: Tailor to recipient's interests
- **Timing**: Consider seasonal themes
- **Packaging**: Add gift bag or basket for multiple jars
- **Cards**: Attach explanation of contents and use
